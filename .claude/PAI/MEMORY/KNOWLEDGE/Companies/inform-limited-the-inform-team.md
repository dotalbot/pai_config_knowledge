## Fit Assessment — Dom's Profile

Dom: 30yr EA, hands-on AI practitioner (Copilot Studio, PowerApps), mission = build real AI value, not PowerPoints.

**Works if:** They want to build a genuine technical delivery practice and need someone to define what EA means there. ADS Group + defence ambitions could be the angle.

**Doesn't work if:** The role is senior adoption strategist dressed up as EA. Dom would be technically overqualified in a company that has no engineering peers and no build motion.

**Key questions to ask:**
1. What % of a typical engagement is hands-on build vs. comms/training/governance?
2. Who builds the technical solution — you, the client, or a partner?
3. Do you hold any Microsoft Solutions Partner designations? If not, why?
4. Is this a permanent employee role or a collaborator engagement?
5. What does the contract pipeline look like for next 12 months?
6. Is the vision to build a technical practice, or is this a senior delivery lead role?
