# Daniel Miessler

Security researcher, writer, and AI commentator. Creator of the Unsupervised Learning newsletter. Known for applying a security-mindset framework to emerging technology — particularly AI adoption and future-of-work analysis.

## Key Contributions

- Articulates the "articulation problem" thesis: the AI capability gap is about human inability to express expertise in structured files, not model limitations
- Proposed the Lattice architecture for enterprise AI context broadcast via a unified daemon
- Coined "Human 3.0" framing — becoming and broadcasting your full self as the human differentiator in an AI world
