# Models

> Mental models I rely on — the frameworks I reach for instinctively.

## Core Models

- **When / Why / What / Where / Who / How** — the six-question frame. Used as a checklist before starting something and as a diagnostic when something's going wrong.

- **Layered architecture with a conveyor belt** — break things into layers, not just top-down but sideways too. Everything has a beginning, middle, and end. Layers have cross-cutting impact on the full lifecycle. The key skill is knowing what's in play at each stage.

- **Multi-move critical thinking** — more than a chessboard. Always consider several moves simultaneously — consequences, dependencies, second-order effects. Never react to just what's in front of you.

---
*Models are the cognitive shortcuts the DA should mirror — when framing analysis or recommendations, these lenses are the ones that will land.*
