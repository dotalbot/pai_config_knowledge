# Ideal State — Freedom

> What autonomy and time actually feel like at their best.

## The Core Feeling

Peace of mind. Settled enough to read a book and absorb it. An afternoon nap. The sound of ocean waves. No background hum of obligation.

## The Place

Somewhere warm. The mental anchor is the north coast of Natal, South Africa — family holidays from childhood. That's the reference point for what freedom feels like physically.

## How Time Is Spent

- With wife and children — exploring, finding new things, no pressure
- Still building and creating — but on his own terms, not someone else's deadline
- Sharing what's been built, handing over, and stepping back when the time is right

## North Star

The freedom to choose — to work because it's interesting, to stop because the day is beautiful, to be fully present without the weight of what needs doing next.
