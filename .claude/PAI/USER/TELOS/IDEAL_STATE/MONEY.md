# Ideal State — Money

> What financial freedom looks like. Targets, not current state.

## Core Target

- Property paid off
- Retirement plan that provides dignity — not dependent on children
- Saving £1,200/month comfortably, without financial stress

## The Feeling

Independent and wealthy enough that losing a job isn't a crisis. Future secured. A "just spend it" fund for enjoyment. Life lived without money anxiety.

## North Star

Not rich for its own sake — just free. Enough that money stops being a background source of dread and becomes a tool instead.
