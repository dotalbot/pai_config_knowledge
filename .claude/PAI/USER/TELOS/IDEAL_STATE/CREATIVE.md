# Ideal State — Creative

> What creative life looks like when it's working.

## How Creativity Works for Dom

Starts with visualisation. If he can see it in his mind, he can usually translate it into reality. Ideas grow while dozing — that half-asleep state is where a lot gets designed.

## What Gets Made

All of the above: physical, digital, written. Electronic projects, woodworking, software, ideas. The medium follows the vision, not the other way around.

## The Space

Home office doubles as a workshop. Currently: too many projects, not enough clear space, ADHD means aspirations outpace organisation. The gap between what's in his head and what he can execute is mostly a space problem, not a skill problem.

## Ideal State

- Everything in its correct place
- A clear desk — or a fast way to clear it
- One physical project at a time, executed properly
- The mental bandwidth to let a thought grow without the environment fighting back

## North Star

A creative life that matches the richness of what's in his head. The ideas are already there — the ideal state is just removing the friction between the idea and the thing.
