# Ideal State — Relationships

> What the most important relationships look like at their best.

## North Star

Nothing left unsaid. Peace and harmony. Live and let live.

## Wife

Fun, laughs, hugs. Loving physical contact and real conversation. A partnership that feels like friendship — light and warm, not heavy.

## Children

- **Son (16), Daughter (18)**
- Love and hugs — always. But the deeper goal: equipping them to find their place in the world, comfortable in themselves, feeling like they belong.
- Not dependency — foundation.

## Close Friends

The ones that count: sharing, love, support. Unbounded by time — time apart doesn't erode it. Pick up exactly where you left off.

## Parents

Present and connected while the time is there.

## Core Principle

The relationships that matter are those where nothing needs performing. You can just be.
